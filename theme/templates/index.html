Elimu Tales

Elimu tales is a fun but educational cartoon series . We seek to use animation as a powerful tool of entertainment in our efforts to make kids smarter. the animation is locally produced and the content is relevant to our target audience. Elimu tales has paid extra attention in Science, Maths and social studies.

The animation is set in a fictional Peri-urban village called Kimbo town . We follow the lives of four young kids Amina, Atieno, Kamau and Kioko. We use their day to day activities to educate children watching the show So far the feedback has been exciting as Elimu tales won the best animation category at the River wood academy awards 2017.
